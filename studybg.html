Study Background

This study aims to display the current situation of higher education in the Luzon Island, the major educational hub of the Philippines, through:


checking whether all the colleges in Luzon are still within the range of the recommended faculty-to-student ratio; and


monitoring the progress of the colleges in Luzon for the S.Y. 2016-2017, S.Y. 2017-2018, and S.Y. 2018-2019 through its student and faculty population.
